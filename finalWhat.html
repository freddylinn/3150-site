What Exactly is Universal Design?

Principles of Universal Design

The Centre for Excellence in Universal Design defines universal design as "the design and composition of an environment so that it can be accessed, understood and used to the greatest extent possible by all people regardless of their age, size, ability or disability." This serves as a strong foundation for what we consider universal design to be as a concept. These pages in particular will focus on universal design on the web, but it can be extended to every part of everyday life. The core idea of designing products that are immediately accessible to all users can guide designers toward a common goal.
